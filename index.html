Classic Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix melted butter and sugar.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla, beat well.
  4. Stir in cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder until combined.
  5. Pour batter into pan and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  6. Let cool before cutting into squares.
